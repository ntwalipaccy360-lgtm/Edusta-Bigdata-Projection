Empowering Rwanda's National
Education System

The Rwanda Ministry of Education leads one of Africa's most ambitious education transformation programs — Vision 2050. EduStat is the data backbone that makes it real.

From primary schools in Musanze to secondary institutions in Kigali, EduStat unifies student performance data across all 30 districts, enabling every education officer to act on evidence — not guesswork.

Nationwide school performance tracking
